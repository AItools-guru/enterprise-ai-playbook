Use Case & User Guide

The Business Use Case

Organizations often struggle to quantify the actual financial impact of AI. This simulator models the real-world operational savings of deploying specialized prompts across a team to automate repetitive daily tasks (like writing PRDs, cargo risk logs, and database indexes).

Step-by-Step Quick Start

  • 1. Adjust Variables: Move the sliders in the left panel (e.g., increase Team Size or Automation %).
  • 2. Audit KPIs: Watch the Net Savings ($) and payback horizon adjust dynamically in the metrics grid.
  • 3. Execute Prompts: Go to the **Prompt Console** tab on the sidebar, select a role, and click **"Simulate AI Execution"** to see live output!
